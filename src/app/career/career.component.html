Career

Eckerds Pharmacy is group of independent pharmacies serving local communities. Our pharmacies employ local residents of the community for various job opportunities.

Career

Following are the positions normally available at our stores.

Designation
Registered Pharmacist
Qualification Required

Candidate should be licensed to practice pharmacy by state board and should be in good standing with Office of Inspector General and CMS.

Designation
Registered Floater Pharmacist
Qualification Required

Candidate should be licensed to practice pharmacy by state board and should be in good standing with Office of Inspector General and CMS. Candidate should be willing to travel.

Designation
Registered Pharmacy Technician
Qualification Required

Candidate should be registered with state board and should be in good standing with Office of Inspector General and CMS.

Designation
Trainee Pharmacy Technician
Qualification Required

Candidate should be willing to learn, open to opportunities and genuinely interested in making pharmacy job a priority. Eckerds will sponsor all tuition cost to provide state approved pharmacy technician training program..

Designation
Compounding Specialist
Qualification Required

Registered Pharmacy Technician can get trained by Eckerds to become compounding specialist. Should be willing to learn and medically fit to handle chemicals. Candidate must agree to follow all OSHA guidelines.

Designation
Delivery Specialist
Qualification Required

Eckerds does employ Delivery Specialist as per needs of specific location. In many cases Registered Pharmacy Technician can be cross trained to perform delivery specialist tasks..
